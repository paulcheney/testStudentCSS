NP Membership — Benefits

No fee for registered non-profit organizations. Benefits include access to targeted networking events, volunteer coordination support, and eligibility for chamber-sponsored grant programs and collaborative initiatives.

Bronze Membership — Benefits

  • Directory listing with contact information
  • Invitations to networking and community events

Bronze gives your organization a reliable presence within the chamber community to build local connections.

Silver Membership — Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits
  • Priority placement in member spotlights
  • Discounted access to training and workshops

Silver is designed for organizations looking to increase visibility and engage more deeply with the business community.

Gold Membership — Benefits

  • All Silver benefits
  • Featured homepage spotlight and sponsored placement
  • Priority access to sponsorships and exclusive events

Gold members receive the highest level of promotional support and partnership opportunities to accelerate growth and leadership presence.
