About Me

Hi — I am Samuel Cortez, a 23-year-old student of Web and Computer Programming. I’m genuinely passionate about computing and technology, and I combine that enthusiasm with practical experience working at a technology company where I help deliver real projects, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and apply industry best practices for readable, maintainable code. My studies have given me a solid foundation in responsive design, front-end development, and accessible interfaces, and on the job I continually sharpen those skills by building user-focused features, debugging complex issues, and learning new tools and frameworks. I enjoy problem solving, improving user experiences, and producing interfaces that balance aesthetics with usability and performance. Outside of tech, I’m a devoted football fan — I love both playing and watching the game because it keeps me active and reinforces teamwork, discipline, and resilience. I also treasure sharing mate and long conversations with family and friends; those relaxed moments recharge me and remind me why I value collaboration and human-centered design. My goal is to grow as a developer, take on increasingly challenging projects, and contribute to products that make people’s lives simpler and more enjoyable.
